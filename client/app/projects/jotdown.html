Weeklyvibes was a passion project between two of my best friends and I. We thought there was an interesting opportunity somewhere within the intersection of concert and music discovery. So, we set out to make a product that allowed someone living in the Bay Area to see the concerts in their area for that week, AND also listen to that musicians top three songs.

What we got was (what we thought, at least) a compelling product. I continue to use it to see what concerts are going around me and to discover (or rediscover) music. The product never took off. We didn’t properly market it, and didn’t iterate on it nearly quickly enough. It’s still live, though. Check it out and let me know what you think :)

I did all of the product design and branding for the project. There are four main components to the interface: day of week filter, venue filter, section content, and event playlist. Because concerts generally happen at night, we rolled with a dark theme. We thought the pink and purple gradient was fun and different, so we stuck with it.
